Our Mission

Research should move at the speed of thought.

At tezt-yan-6, we build Researchily — the AI-native workspace designed specifically for the rigorous demands of R&D labs and innovation teams.

Why we exist.

Generic AI tools are built for the general public. They "hallucinate" technical details, lack scientific depth, and often ignore the critical need for verifiable citations. In an R&D environment, a single misdirected experiment can waste millions in budget and months of human effort.


We saw researchers spending more time skimming thousands of papers and manually organizing experiment plans than actually innovating. We decided to bridge the gap between high-level intelligence and scientific accuracy.

The Researchily Difference

Unlike standard chatbots, Researchily is grounded in peer-reviewed literature and patent databases. We don't just provide answers; we provide a transparent audit trail of where those answers came from, ensuring your intellectual property and experimental designs are built on a bedrock of truth.
