Our Story

Frustrated by the "Pilot Purgatory"

For too long, enterprise AI has been characterized by two extremes: vendors selling impossible sci-fi visions, or internal teams stuck in a perpetual cycle of low-value pilots that never reach production.

We saw CTOs and CAIOs struggling to justify budgets to skeptical boards. We saw massive investments in LLMs that failed to move a single needle on the quarterly P&L. We knew there was a better way.

tezt-yan-6 was built to provide the "Middle Path": a structured, engineering-led approach to strategy that prioritizes high-impact use cases that deliver measurable ROI in 90 days.
